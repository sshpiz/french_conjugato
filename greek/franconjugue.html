Greek Verb Mnemonics & Tips

-ER Verbs Mnemonic

Mnemonic: e, es, e, ons, ez, ent

Rhyme: “Je -e, Tu -es, Il -e... Nous -ons, Vous -ez, Ils -ent.”

Example: parler → je parle, tu parles, il parle, nous parlons...

-IR Verbs Mnemonic

Mnemonic: is, is, it, issons, issez, issent

Phrase: "IS, IS, IT – ISSONS, ISSEZ, ISSENT"

Example: finir → je finis, tu finis, il finit, nous finissons...

-RE Verbs Mnemonic

Mnemonic: s, s, –, ons, ez, ent

Note: “Il/Elle gets nothing — they’re shy.”

Example: vendre → je vends, tu vends, il vend, nous vendons...

Boot Verbs (Stem-changing)

Mnemonic: Think of a boot shape covering forms: je, tu, il/elle, ils/elles

Rule: Stem changes apply inside the boot, but not to nous and vous.

Example: acheter → j’achète, tu achètes, nous achetons
