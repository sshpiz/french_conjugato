Why verbs first?

Verbs are where grammar becomes action. They carry person, time, mood, and a huge amount of the feeling of fluency. Practicing them in context makes reading, listening, and speaking noticeably less fragile.

Shared features

  • Flashcards by tense, pronoun, and frequency tier.
  • Usage examples that make meanings less abstract.
  • Explorer view for full tables and searching.
  • Offline-friendly installable web apps.
