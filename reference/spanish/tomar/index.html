Usages & examples

tomar + objetoToma este documento y revísalo.Take this document and look it over.
tomar + bebida / comidaTomamos un café antes de empezar.We had a coffee before starting.
tomar + transporteTomo el autobús de las siete.I take the seven o'clock bus.
tomar en serioNadie tomó en serio su advertencia.Nobody took his warning seriously.
